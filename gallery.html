Hello! Welcome to Rolipoli Gallery

Camping can be an exhausting activity: tiring, both physically and emotionally, when adventurers are out of their comfortable element. Although the needs of those adventurers with limited ability in their upper limbs drove the product development, Rolipoli adds ease to the process of setting up camp for adventurers of all abilitiesnumerous adventurers.


A persona, named Undaunted Austin, embodied the needs of several adventurers, as a survivor of shoulder disarticulation (an amputation of the upper limb at the shoulder) and a champion of the outdoors. To conserve his strength, Austin travels to campgrounds by car, and he stores the Rolipoli in the trunk of the vehicle.


The Rolipoli is durable enough to survive a bumpy car ride as well as lightweight, making it easy to carry. Though the Rolipoli is conveyed by car, the materials of its construction are lightweight, despite its outward appearance, for situations when the adventurer prefers to carry the product in their hand.


Adhesive patches of velcro material are included in Rolipoli's accessories kit, allowing adventurers to take their favorite sleeping gear on their journey. Adventurers can apply the patches to their favorite sleeping bag to make it compatible with Rolipoli. Once inside the Rolipoli, the sleeping bagitem will be protected from the elements. A majority of the product is wrapped in ripstop nylon, which will keep comfort items dry in wet conditions protected, and protect blankets and sleeping bags from thorny plants and rough branches. The underside of Rolipoli is wrapped in a heavy canvas material that traps dirt and forms to uneven ground conditions while keeping sleeping gear clean and comfortable. When Austin is ready to rest, he puts down the Rolipoli on a clear area, grabs hold of the end of the sleeping bagroll's end and pulls slowly, until his sleeping bag has extended to its full length and the attachment cloth is exposed.
